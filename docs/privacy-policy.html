Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 16, 2026

Geography Quiz ("the App") is developed and maintained by the app developer ("we", "us", or "our"). This privacy policy explains how the App handles your information.

Information We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers. All data remains on your device.

The following data is stored locally on your device only:

  • Quiz scores and completion history
  • Achievement progress
  • App settings and preferences (e.g., timer, hard mode)
  • Player name (if you choose to set one)

Google Play Games Services

The App integrates with Google Play Games Services for achievements. When you use this feature, Google may collect data in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. We do not have access to your Google account information beyond what is required for achievements functionality.

Third-Party Services

The App does not include any advertising, analytics, or tracking services. No data is shared with third parties, except as described above for Google Play Games Services.

Internet Access

The App can function fully offline. Internet access is only used for Google Play Games Services features (achievements and sign-in).

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. The App is an educational geography quiz suitable for all ages.

Data Deletion

All app data is stored locally on your device. You can delete all data at any time by clearing the app's data in your device settings or by uninstalling the App.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the app's GitHub repository.
